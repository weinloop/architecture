## 1）跟读原文（建议读 3 遍）

Cloud-native observability helps teams understand how services behave in production. It combines **metrics**, **logs**, and **traces** to answer three questions: *Is the system healthy? Why is it slow or failing? Which change caused the issue?* Instead of relying on a single dashboard, modern platforms correlate signals across services and infrastructure. With structured logging, distributed tracing, and well-designed service-level objectives, engineers can detect incidents early, reduce mean time to recovery, and make performance trade-offs explicit. In practice, the goal is not to collect more data, but to collect the right data and turn it into actionable decisions.
